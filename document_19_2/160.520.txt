# Law
160.520
# Key Legal Concepts
Subpoena: A legal document that orders an individual to attend a hearing and potentially produce evidence. Administrative Law Judge (ALJ): An official who presides over hearings and makes decisions regarding the issuance of subpoenas.
# Key Legal Principles
Due Process: The requirement that all parties have the opportunity to present their case, which includes the ability to compel witness testimony. Material Evidence: Evidence that is relevant and significant to the case being presented at the hearing.
# Application Scenarios
A party in a hearing may require the testimony of a witness whose information is crucial for their argument. A respondent may seek to quash a subpoena if they believe it is unjust or burdensome.
