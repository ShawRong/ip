# Law
160.522
# Key Legal Concepts
Subpoena: A legal document that orders an individual to appear as a witness or produce evidence in a legal proceeding. Witness Fees: Compensatory payments made to witnesses for their time and travel incurred while attending a legal proceeding.
# Key Legal Principles
Cost Responsibility: The party that requests a subpoena is responsible for covering the associated costs of witness fees and mileage, establishing a principle of accountability in legal proceedings. Exemption for Government Entities: When a subpoena is issued on behalf of the Secretary, the requirement for accompanying witness fees is waived, highlighting a distinction in the treatment of government entities compared to private parties.
# Application Scenarios
A party in a legal dispute issues a subpoena to a witness, ensuring they provide a check for fees when serving the subpoena. A government agency issues a subpoena for a witness without needing to provide upfront fees, streamlining the process for governmental investigations.
