# Law
164.502(a)(2)
# Key Legal Concepts
***Covered Entities***: Organizations or individuals that handle protected health information (PHI), such as healthcare providers, health plans, and healthcare clearinghouses. ***Protected Health Information (PHI)***: Any individually identifiable health information that is transmitted or maintained in any form or medium, which is protected under the law.
# Key Legal Principles
***Required Disclosures***: Certain situations mandate that covered entities disclose PHI without patient consent, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. ***Compliance Obligations***: Covered entities must adhere to specific regulations, including responding to requests from individuals and governmental authorities as stipulated in the law.
