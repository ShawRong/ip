# Law
160.530(d)
# Key Legal Concepts
Sanctions: Refers to penalties imposed by the Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) for non-compliance with orders or procedures, or for misconduct. Due Process: Ensures that parties involved in a hearing have the right to a fair and orderly process, which includes the ability to defend their actions.
# Key Legal Principles
Proportionality: Sanctions must be proportionate to the severity and nature of the failure or misconduct, ensuring fairness in enforcement. Judicial Discretion: The ALJ has the authority to determine the appropriate sanctions based on the specific circumstances of each case.
# Application Scenarios
An attorney fails to submit required documents before a hearing, resulting in the ALJ imposing a fine or other disciplinary action. A party repeatedly interrupts proceedings and disrupts the order of the hearing, prompting the ALJ to issue a warning or impose sanctions to restore order.
