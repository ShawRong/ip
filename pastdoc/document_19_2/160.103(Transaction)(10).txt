# Law
160.103(Transaction)(10)
# Key Legal Concepts
Transaction: The act of transmitting information between two parties for financial or administrative activities in healthcare, emphasizing communication and data exchange. Health claims attachments: Documents or additional information provided to support health claims, critical for the processing and approval of claims.
# Key Legal Principles
Confidentiality: Ensuring that sensitive health information is securely transmitted and protected during transactions. Accuracy: The requirement that all transmitted information, including claims attachments, must be accurate to prevent fraud and ensure proper processing.
# Application Scenarios
Submitting a claim for reimbursement, where health claims attachments are necessary to provide supporting documentation. Transmitting administrative data between healthcare providers and insurers, where both parties require accurate and timely information.
# Relationship to Overall Document Argument
Establishes the importance of clear and defined processes for health information transactions, which are essential for effective healthcare administration. Highlights the legal requirements surrounding health claims attachments as a component of broader regulatory compliance in health care transactions.
