# Law
160.103(Modify%20or%20modification)
# Key Legal Concepts
Modification: Refers to any changes made to existing standards or specifications as authorized by the Secretary. Regulatory Authority: The power vested in the Secretary to implement changes through regulations.
# Key Legal Principles
Authority of the Secretary: The principle that the Secretary has the legal power to modify standards or specifications. Regulatory Framework: Changes must comply with established rules and procedures within the subchapter.
# Application Scenarios
Updating healthcare standards to improve patient data privacy in response to new technologies. Revising implementation specifications for electronic health records to accommodate new interoperability standards.
# Relationship to Overall Document Argument
Establishes the framework for how standards can evolve in response to changing needs or technologies. Supports the overarching goal of maintaining relevant and effective regulations within the subchapter.
