# Law
164.502(g)(5)(ii)
# Key Legal Concepts
Covered Entity: A person or organization that provides healthcare services and is subject to privacy regulations under HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act). Personal Representative: An individual who has the authority to act on behalf of another person in legal matters, particularly concerning health information.
# Key Legal Principles
Professional Judgment: The discretion exercised by healthcare providers to determine what is in the best interest of their patients, particularly in sensitive situations involving abuse, neglect, or endangerment. Best Interest of the Individual: A guiding principle that prioritizes the individual's welfare and safety over the rights of their personal representative when abuse or neglect is suspected.

# Relationship to Overall Document Argument

The inclusion of professional discretion reinforces the need for healthcare providers to assess situations on a case-by-case basis, particularly in contexts involving potential harm.
