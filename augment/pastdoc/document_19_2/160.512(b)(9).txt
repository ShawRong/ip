# Law
160.512(b)(9)
# Key Legal Concepts
The role of Administrative Law Judges (ALJs) in conducting hearings and managing prehearing conferences. The importance of setting the time and place for hearings to ensure procedural fairness and adequate notice.
# Key Legal Principles
The principle of due process, which ensures that all parties have a fair opportunity to present their case. The principle of administrative efficiency, which promotes the effective use of resources in managing hearings.
# Application Scenarios
Scheduling hearings in cases involving disputes over administrative decisions. Facilitating discussions in prehearing conferences to streamline proceedings and clarify issues.
# Relationship to Overall Document Argument
Establishing a clear timeline and location for hearings supports the overall integrity of the administrative process. Prehearing conferences allow for the resolution of logistical issues, enhancing the effectiveness of the hearing process.
