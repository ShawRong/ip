# Law
164.510(a)(1)(ii)(B)
# Key Legal Concepts
Directory purposes: This refers to the use or disclosure of certain information for the purpose of maintaining a directory of individuals, typically within a healthcare or institutional setting. Religious affiliation: This is a specific type of information that is protected from disclosure under the law, emphasizing the sensitivity and privacy associated with an individual's religious beliefs.
# Key Legal Principles
Individual consent: The principle that individuals should have control over their personal information, including how and when it is disclosed to others. Protection of sensitive information: The legal obligation to safeguard specific types of sensitive information, such as religious affiliation, from being disclosed without proper authorization.
