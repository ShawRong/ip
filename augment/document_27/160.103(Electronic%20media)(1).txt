# Law
160.103(Electronic%20media)(1)
# Key Legal Concepts
Electronic storage material: Refers to any medium that can store data electronically, including hard drives and removable storage devices. Data recording: The process of saving information in electronic form on various storage mediums.
# Key Legal Principles
Ownership of electronic data: Establishes who has the rights to the data stored on electronic media. Data privacy and protection: Pertains to legal obligations regarding the safeguarding of data stored on electronic media.
# Application Scenarios
Legal disputes over data ownership when electronic media is transferred between parties. Cases involving data breaches where information stored electronically is improperly accessed.
# Relationship to Overall Document Argument
Clarifies the definition of electronic media within the context of data regulation and ownership. Supports broader discussions on privacy laws and the legal implications of data storage.
