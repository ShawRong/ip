### Step 1: Check the HIPAA Regulation IDs and Their Content

The HIPAA Privacy Rule (45 CFR Part 160 and Part 164) protects individuals' medical records and other personal health information. Key components include:

- **Protected Health Information (PHI)**: Any information about health status, provision of healthcare, or payment for healthcare that can be linked to an individual.
- **Permitted Disclosures**: PHI can be disclosed without patient consent in certain circumstances, such as for public health activities, legal proceedings, or when required by law.
