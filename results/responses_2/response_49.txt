### Step 1: Check the HIPAA Regulation IDs

**Regulation ID 164.502(g)(5)(ii)**  
This regulation addresses the circumstances under which a covered entity may disclose protected health information (PHI) to a personal representative of an individual. It stipulates that the covered entity must ensure that the personal representative is acting on behalf of the individual and has the authority to do so under applicable law.

**Regulation ID 164.502(a)(5)(ii)(B)(2)(viii)**  
This regulation defines the conditions under which health information can be disclosed without consent, including situations where the information is pertinent to the legal representation of the individual, provided that the attorney is recognized as a personal representative.
