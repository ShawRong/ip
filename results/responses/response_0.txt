### Step 1: Check HIPAA Regulation IDs

**Regulation ID 164.504(a): Summary Health Information**

- **Summary Health Information** is defined under HIPAA as information that summarizes the claims history, claims experience, or type of health coverage provided to individuals, but excludes any identifying information (such as names or Social Security numbers).
- The regulation allows for the use of Summary Health Information for purposes such as obtaining premium bids or modifying, amending, or terminating a group health plan.
